How to make Homemade Pasta

This is the best recipe for pasta

Here is where you are going to lean how to make the best pasta ever! Check out the ingredients and get your hands dirty!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Olive oil
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Mix the flour with the water and olive oil
  2. Add the basil
  3. Let it rest
  4. Bake it
