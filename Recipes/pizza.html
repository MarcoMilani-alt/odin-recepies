How to make Pizza

This is the best recipe for pizza

Here is where you are going to lean how to make the best pizza ever! Check out the ingredients and get your hands dirty!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Olive oil
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Mix the flour with the water and olive oil
  2. Add the cheese to the mixture
  3. Let it rest
  4. Bake it
