How to make Bread

This is the best recipe for Bread

Here is where you are going to lean how to make the best bread ever! Check out the ingredients and get your hands dirty!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Olive oil
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Mix the flour with the water and olive oil
  2. Add the basil to the mixture
  3. Let it rest
  4. Bake it
